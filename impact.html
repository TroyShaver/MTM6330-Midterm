The Culture

The culture has been massively influenced by streetwear. Speaking as an avid Supreme fan myself, I believe it has really put the world on its back, as to what defines fashion. The abstract work of so many artists on Supreme streetwear has left an imprint on value, marketing, and business of fashion.

The outpour of hiphop artists, R&B artists etc. who value Supreme is indescriable. It is a wonderful clothing brand that everyone wants to see excel and continue to dominate the market. It has garnered millions upon millions of dollars in sales, and will continue to do so. It's sole impact on the culture does not go unnoticed!

Personal Impact

I hope to one day start my own clothing business. I have begun working on styles, and I always keep Supreme at the back of my head. The constant need for innovating with fashion comes from the sole passion of clothing in the first place. Expressing yourself through clothing that carries its personality on its own is something Supreme never lacked.
